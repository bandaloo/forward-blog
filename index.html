Forward is a game about recovery. Inspired by real life events, Forward takes you through a surreal journey, recovering physically and mentally in a hospital after a life threatening accident.

Forward was created for Game Design Studio at WPI. You can watch our trailer and read our weekly dev log on this site. Visit our itch.io page to download the game and play it for yourself.
